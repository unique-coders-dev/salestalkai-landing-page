Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At SalesTalkAi, your privacy is our top priority. We build tools that handle your business negotiations, and we understand the level of trust that requires. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the SalesTalkAi Chrome extension, we collect the following:

  • Google Profile Data: Name and email address when you authenticate via Google OAuth.
  • Platform Context: Temporary text buffers from your active chats on supported platforms (e.g., Fiverr, Upwork) strictly during the moment you click "Generate".

2. How We Use Your Data

The temporary conversational context is sent to our AI models (like OpenAI or Groq) strictly for generating your requested reply. We do not use your private client messages to train our public AI models, nor do we store them permanently in our databases.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption for all data transit. Your Google authentication tokens are processed securely and your billing data is handled exclusively by validated third-party processors (like Stripe).

4. Third-Party Access

We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information or chat logs to third parties. Data is only shared with our infrastructure providers to facilitate the generation of AI responses under strict confidentiality guarantees.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of your account and any associated profile data at any time by contacting our support team.
